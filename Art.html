shining night

This art project about a very relaxed mood and how the colors can help people become relaxed while looking at the project we have created.

The shining night project was an project that contains 9 people who was chose to come up with a mood that everyone that loves to feel and the whole group chose to do a very relaxing feeling.With this feeling we came up with making a fake sky in the hallway that has stars,moons,a sun, and very relaxing colors and we chose this because we wanted people to feel very relaxed once they stepped foot in that part of the hallway and we wanyed people to feel relax once they looked up at the stars,moon, aslo at the very cool colors.

Sound Waves

The sound wave project was to express your feeling and thoughts without writing them down,but by the pitch and waves of your voice gives off.

This sound wave project was consisted of wood,paint,and paint strips.The sound waves project was kind of hard because you had to trace your sound waves off the paper, then yoy had to trace the waves onto the wood board.Once the waves was printed on the wood you have to neatly paint the lines with a specific color scheme and my colors were white and red in order to express my feelings between love and hate.
